About NCC

The NCC is a vibrant youth organization, which has made a commendable contribution in producing responsible and patriotic citizens. From a small beginning, today it has grown to the largest youth organization. NCC motivates and trains upcoming generations to render their meaningful contribution towards national integration and development.

Organizational Details

DG NCC: New Delhi under Ministry of Defence Govt of India
State NCC Directorate: HR.HP PB & CHD
NCC Group HQ: Ambala
NCC Battalion: Kurukshetra (Army), Karnal (Air Force), Faridabad (Navy)
Institute: National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra (Haryana)

NCC Camps

It is compulsory for cadets to attend at least two NCC training camps, each lasting about 10 days in the 2nd and 3rd year. The camps expose cadets to a disciplined, regimental lifestyle and physical/mental challenges, enhancing personality development.

Exams

Eligibility for 'B' Certificate Exam:
● 75% attendance in 1st-year parades
● 75% attendance in 2nd-year parades
● Must have attended one NCC training camp in the 2nd year

Eligibility for 'C' Certificate Exam:
● Must have passed 'B' certificate exam
● 75% attendance in 3rd-year parades
● Must have attended one NCC Annual Training Camp (ATC) in the 3rd year

NCC certificates ('B' & 'C') hold great value for career advancement.

Financial Assistance

NCC provides financial assistance in the form of scholarships for meritorious and needy students. These scholarships are awarded by the DG NCC and other organizations to support cadets in their educational journey.
